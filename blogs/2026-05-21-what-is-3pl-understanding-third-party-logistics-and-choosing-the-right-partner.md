TL;DR Summary A third-party logistics (3PL) provider runs warehousing, fulfillment, transportation, and returns on behalf of a brand — so the brand doesn't have to own the trucks, warehouses, or labor. 3PLs fall into four working categories: warehouse-based, transportation-based, financial/IT-based, and full-service — each solves a different problem. The right 3PL is decided by network density, technology depth, pricing transparency, and SLA discipline — not by who's largest. A 3PL handles the physical movement of goods. A logistics intelligence platform like ClickPost — connecting 600+ carriers worldwide and processing millions of monthly shipments — sits on top, unifying tracking, NDR, returns, and customer communication across every 3PL and carrier you use.
