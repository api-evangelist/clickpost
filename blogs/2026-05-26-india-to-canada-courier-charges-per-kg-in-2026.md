TL;DR — India to Canada Courier Charges in 2026 Express courier charges to Canada start at ₹1,650 for 500g and ₹1,790 for 1 kg , with 3–5 day delivery via DHL, FedEx, UPS, or Aramex. Economy services start at ₹1,490 for 500g and ₹1,499 for 1 kg , with 8–14 day delivery via Canada Post or sea-air hybrid lanes. Bulk shipments above 10 kg drop to ₹500–₹600/kg express and ₹280–₹500/kg economy , with steeper discounts above 50 kg. Add 18% GST + 15–25% fuel surcharge to all base rates. Remote-area surcharges apply for non-metro destinations. Cheapest is Canada Post economy for sub-5 kg parcels; fastest is DHL Express at 3–4 business days door-to-door. Customs duty is paid by the recipient under DDU shipping. DDP improves delivery success but adds 10–18% to landed cost.
