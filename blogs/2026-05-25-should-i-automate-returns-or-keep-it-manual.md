TL;DR Summary Automate returns if volume is rising, your team is stuck on repetitive checks, or refunds and inventory updates keep slipping. Stay manual only when volume is small and every case needs human judgment. For most growing brands, the right answer is hybrid — automate the routine, keep humans on the exceptions.
